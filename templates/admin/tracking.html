Observer Pattern - Real-time Alerts

This tracking system uses the Observer Pattern to notify administrators when vehicles go out of range:

  • Subject: Tracking Service monitors vehicle locations
  • Observers: Admin Notifier + Alert Logger receive notifications
  • Trigger: When distance exceeds 50km from rental location
  • Action: Automatic alerts sent and logged
